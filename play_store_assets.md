## 🔒 Privacy Policy Template

**Effective Date: July 14, 2026**

FoodieGo (the "App") is developed as an Android application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal details you share with us.

### 1. Information Collection and Use
We collect the following personal details to provide and improve our service:
*   **Full Name & Email**: Used to create your secure user account profile.
*   **Phone Number & Addresses**: Used strictly to arrange delivery services for placed orders.
*   **Profile Pictures**: Uploaded securely to Firebase Storage to customize your account avatar.

### 2. Data Storage
All data is stored securely using:
*   **Firebase Authentication** for user password credentials.
*   **Cloud Firestore** for user profile records and address lists.
*   **Firebase Storage** for profile photo assets.
*   **Local SQLite SharedPreferences** for offline caching.

### 3. Data Protection
Your personal data is never shared, sold, or rented to third-party services or marketing brokers. We implement industry-standard database security rules to protect user information.

### 4. Contact Us
If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via support@foodiego.com.
